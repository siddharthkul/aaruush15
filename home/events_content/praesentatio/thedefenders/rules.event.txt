<h4>Report the unpredictable</h4>
<ul>
  <li>A member of the team will report and the other will record</li>
  <li>Recording to be done on spot depending on the topic</li>
  <li>Time limit of 1 minute per card</li>
  <li>Over all 6-7 minutes per team</li>
</ul>
<h4>Walk the Talk</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Simultaneous physical task and debate on the topic to be done</li>
  <li>Cannot stop physical task  in between(no breaks)</li>
  <li>Both the teammates have to perform(for/Against topic accordingly)</li>
</ul>
<h4> Spin your defence </h4>
<ul>
<li>Case study will be given with starting and end.Creatively the story to be woven</li>
<li> Time frame to be taken into account(1.5-2 hrs)</li>
<li> Including 6 specified people in the case study(unknown to the team) </li>
</ul>
