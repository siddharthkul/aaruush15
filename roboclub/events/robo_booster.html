ROBO BOOSTER


Introduction

An endurance race filled with variety of obstacles and breath taking adventurous tasks followed by an essence of victory is all what this event is about.

Participants with their bots are tested thoroughly throughout the race and based on the final results winner is taken.

Round description

This race starts with an path filled with twists and turns followed by climbing the Everest. The race goes on by pushing the stones into the pits which is obstructing your path. We have an ramp followed by an area of sand where the bot has to travel the whole of the desert ( being the toughest endurance of the whole marathon). The race will end with bursting a ballon with the needle placed on the apex of the bot. We get our essence of the victory.

Rules and regulations
  1. All the bots are manually operated.
  2. They can be wired or wireless.
  3. The power system of the bot should be mounted on the system itself.
  4. Motor specification: 600 rpm and a maximum of 4 motors can be used.
  5. Dimensions of the bot :30*30*30 cm
  6. The bot should be assembled by the participant and it shouldnt be an bought model.
  7. Any damage on or of the arena by the bot, the participant will be held responsible.
  8. The participant should not damage the arena.
Rules Regarding Arena
  1. Only two chances would be given if the bot goes off the track.
  2. Participant is tested on the same track until the final winner list is sorted out.
  3. It is purely time based event and there are no negative points in the track.
  4. Penalities would be given if the participant does not bind to the rules.
