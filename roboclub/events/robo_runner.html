Robotics Club

ROBO RUNNER


Introduction

You have to design a robot either wired or wireless that can travel through all turns of the track. The robot that will complete the specified task in least time will be the winner.

Rounds
  • The First round of our event includes a track where the participant have to run your robot. It should be proper enough to cross the sharp turns, ups and downs. To qualify, the team just need to stay on track and focus on driving the robot, this will justify how accurate the robot maneuvers through the track.
  • In Second round the robot has to show its racing skills like sprint. There will be many light spot (Checkpoints) glowing one at a time where the robot has to rush. The participant (or the team) will not know where the next spot will be. The team needs to cross as many lights as possible in the given time limit. Judgment will be based on the max LED's crossed within limited time. The track will have some ups and downs.
  • This is Third round (Jackpot Round). As the name shows it needs faith in your creation in your robot. The arena will be with lots of hurdles, objections like rough path with pebbles, speed breakers, oscillating rods, ups-downs, etc. The participant needs to focus on track and to qualify you need to pass through all objections.
Rules
  • If the robot goes off-track then only one fall over will be given. Penalty [some seconds will be added to elapsed time taken]
  • You can reverse your bot max 2 or 3 times (depending upon the number of contestants).
  • Elimination will be done on the total time taken by the bot to complete the challenge.
  • The bots who take less time to complete the track will move on to the next round.
  • Number of contestants to be disqualified after the completion of each round will depend on the total number of participants. And the number will be announced on the day of the event.
  • Each bot will be provided 3 mins time inside an arena having led lightings. Bot who touches the light the maximum number of time as the led glows will be the first to qualify for the final round.
  • Bot going outside the arena will be awarded huge penalty.
  • The glowing time of the led will be according to the size of the arena and the bot that touches the maximum light source (LED) within the time given will be qualified.
  • No fall over will be provided.
  • Bot can be reversed a limited number of times and accordingly some seconds will be added to the total time taken.
  • The bot which takes the least amount of time to complete the track will be declared as the winner.
