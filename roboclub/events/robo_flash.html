ROBOFLASH


Introduction

Well this is a competition comprising all terrain time lap race. Well it seems simple but don’t judge it so easily, though a single race, but its what where the toughness lies. We will not so easily let u pass by, first pass through us, then comes the actual game.

Rounds

The race is completed only if the robot turns two switches for the led lights and return to the start point. The track will be one round containg rough terrain with sharp breakers ,oil spills , gravel pitch, muddy roads, plain roads and narrow roads.

The race will start with sharp breakers and the bots will truly jump high but the roads will be narrow somewhere and somewhere broad all is that u need to control it. And then ur bot will be on a oil spilled road and after that u will reach ur first checkpoint, u need to switch on the led light over there.

The next lap starts with a relaxing road as the road will be a highway type, well we can’t always keep u relaxed, as the roads will again narrowed, and then starts the gravel pitched road then comes the next point i.e., the second led light switch on and return to the starting point, don’t think returning will be so easy, the roads will be a bit tough, well to be frank it will be muddy cross and win.

The foremost thing it will be TIME LAP RACE, one takes less time easily takes the trophy.

The bot goes out of the edge of the track u will be disqualified then and there.

The width of the road will be 50cms(normal) and 36cms(narrow)

The total length of the road will be 1200 cms

300( sharp breakers), 250(oil spilled), 200(plain road), 250(gravelled road), 200(muddy road).

RoBot Specification
  1. Maximum 30x30x30 cubic cms dimension.
  2. Motor restriction of 1000 rpm.
  3. No additional attachments permitted.
  4. Weight limit of 5kg ( including weight of battery).
  5. No IC engines allowed.
  6. Only one 12v, 7.2Ah battery.
  7. Operation of the bot can be wired or wireless.
