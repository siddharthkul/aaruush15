Robotics Club

ROBOBASH


Introduction

Remember the race held in the annual functions in school time where we had to hold a spoon in our mouth with a lemon on top, run as fast as possible without the lemon falling. Let the kid in us come into existence once again and come be part of the lemon race but on a totally different level. There is no running, skipping or jumping here but all we need is patience, practise and better techniques.

So get your batteries charged, motors aligned and be part of the next edition of the same old lemon race.

Round description

Stones, mud, gravel, bricks and a long way till the finish line. Now this is not it, how can we forget the surprise element being added on the track. Gear yourself up and participate in the fun filled lemon race once again, reliving your childhood days.

Game Rules
  1. The lemon should not fall off the spoon at any time when the robot is making its way through the track.
  2. A penalty of 5 seconds will be added, each time the lemon falls down.
  3. After replacing the lemon on the spoon, the bot has to start from the last checkpoint crossed on the track.
Judgement

Cross the finish line in the least time and the game is yours.

Robot Specification
  1. Maximum 30cmx30cmx30cm in dimension.
  2. Motor restriction of 500 rpm.
  3. No additional attachments permitted.
  4. Weight limit of 5kg (including weight of battery).
  5. No IC engines allowed.
  6. Only one 12v, 7.2Ah battery permitted.
  7. Operation can be wired or wireless.
