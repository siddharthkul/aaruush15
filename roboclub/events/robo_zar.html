Robotics Club

RoboCzar


Introduction

Robozar is all about clash of Robos in two different levels. First they have to show there muscle Power and fighting skills in a street wrestling. Second round is soccer buster where robos have to show there team work and how fast are they along with their power. In short the winning bot must be strong, fast and have a skilled Operator.

Rounds
  1. Sumo WrestlingThis round will include a circle pitch where two robots will be fighting with each other and the one which strikes out the other from the ring first wins the game. Not only power but speed and tactic is also very important to win this round. Top 8 best bots with all their skills will move to the second round.
  2. Soccer bustingThis round is based on the platform of soccer but in this instead of kicking ball bots have to burst the balloons. The Game will include 2 teams with 2 bots each. In first round one of the bot will be in defensive mode and other one will be in striking mode in each team and in second round bots are going to change their mode. There will be 5 balloons each and the team which will burst all the five balloons first will win the game.
Rules
  • Robots should be manual.
  • Wired/wireless.
  • It is not allowed to touch the robot once the game is started.
  • Participants should properly check their robot before the game starts.
Bot Specification
  1. 12v battery
  2. Dimension 30*30*30
  3. 500-1000 rpm
