Robotics Club

ROBO SPRINT


Introduction

This is a event based on the concept of racing. Sprint is a single lapped racing event. It tests the speed management of the bot with given specifications and the control of the controller on the bot.

Rounds
  1. ROUND 1 (SPEED): This round is called SPEED. This round is a maze solving round. In this round a team must solve the maze and take the bot from start point to the end point. The whole maze will be visible to the participants as they will be at a higher position. Times for completion of the maze will be noted. One team will be called to the arena at a time and will have only one chance to have a go at it. There will be checkpoints which only the timer knows and will note the time for reaching that particular checkpoint.
  2. ROUND 2 (SPRINT): This round is a racing round. As the name suggests this round will be a single lapped race. The top ten teams from the round 1 will be qualified into this round. These ten teams will be divided into two batches. Each batch with 5 teams. Race will be held for each batch individually. The top three finishers of batch one and the top two from the batch two will be qualified into the final race from which only one participant wins the event.
Rules

GAME: 3 members per team

BOT SPECIFICATIONS

  • Manual controlled robots only
  • Wired or wireless
  • 30*30*30 in cm
  • 2 motors (600 rpm or less)
  • Three 12 volts battery
  • Bot weight must be less than 5kgs
  • Bot should be a student made bot not a purchased one
Evaluation Scheme
  1. In the first round time taken to complete the check point will be noted.
  2. If the participant will not be able to complete the maze then time taken to complete the checkpoints will be considered.
  3. In second round if the bot goes out of the specified track or hits the side lines there will be a time penalty
