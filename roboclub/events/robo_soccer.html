Robotics Club

ROBO SOCCER


Introduction

Soccer fans taking the game to the next level with robots. A competition comprising of a player, a ball and rectangle post. The only thing replacing would be a player by a robot.

Rounds
  1. The kick point will be on the on the start point of the field.
  2. The first round comprises of robots made by the participants taking free shoots to the goal post from different angles and positions, the goal would have no keeper. Minimum of six should be scored out of 10 to qualify for the next round.
  3. The second round comprises of robots made by the participants going head to head, one on one, robot to robot not fights, but one on one soccer game. In this round the fixture would be randomly decided.
Rules
  • The ball will be a standard tennis ball.
  • The field will be of (250x65) sq. cms .
  • The goal post will be of Round 1: 15 x 10. Round 2: 25x15 (sq. cms).
  • The time limit given for the one on one round would be mins.
Robot Specification
  1. Maximum 30x30x30 cubic cms dimension.
  2. Motor restriction of 500 rpm.
  3. No additional attachments permitted.
  4. Weight limit of 5kg ( including weight of battery).
  5. No IC engines allowed.
  6. Only one 12v, 7.2Ah battery.
  7. Operation can be wired or wireless.
