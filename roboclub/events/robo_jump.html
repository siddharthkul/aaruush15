Robotics Club

ROBOJUMP


Introduction

The following is a sports event based on the game Long Jump.

In this event competitors jump as far as possible along the ground in one leap and the competitor is expected to combine speed, strength, and agility in an attempt to leap as far as possible from a take off point.

Rounds

This event is a single round event in which the team would be provided with a plank which they are supposed to arrange in such a way that the bot can jump as far as possible in a single attempt or leap.

Along with the plank bricks would be provided so that the team can efficiently adjust the height of the plank and required configuration (Angle of inclination).

Rules

The judging criteria are based upon two parameters which are

  1. The distance which the bot covers over the leap, the longer the distance the more are the points gained by the team.
  2. The angle of the plank which is made by the individual or group.
  3. The points scored by the team would be based on the addition of both the distance covered as well as the angle which was chosen for the plank.
    For example: - if distance (D) is 100 cm and the angle (B) made is 40 degree
    Total points= D + A
    Where A=180(degree)-B
    So the points would be 240.
  4. The bot specification is 30cm*30cm*30cm
  5. Maximum motor speed would be 1000 rpm
  6. Maximum voltage : 12V DC
