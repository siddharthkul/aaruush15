ROBO MOON BUZZER


Introduction

This event is based on the game 'don't touch the line' and a round of ATV track. Wanna know more...come and enjoy the game.

Rounds

  1. It's all about ATV and motocross.

    In this round your bot will have to overcome many obstacles and move on till the finish line. The obstacles will be small pits(similar to moon surface), oil, grease, a bridge, fine sand, pebbles and a big pit. The shape of track will be similar to 8.

  2. Line may scream

    As the name goes...stay away for the line it may scream out loud...only if u try to touch it. There will be a loop provided to be attached on your bot with it you have to cross the track with line passing through your loop. The ground in this track will be level ground.

Just A Skectch For Rounds
Rules
  1. The bot has to be manual.
  2. The bot must be of 30*30*30 cm in dimensions (bot should not exceed this dimension at any time during the race)
  3. The battery use Should not exceed 12V
  4. Motor - max 1000 rpm
  5. Bot can be wired or wireless
  6. A team should be of maximum 3 members
