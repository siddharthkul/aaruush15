Robotics Club

FAQ

How does Robotics club function?
The club works in tandem with AARUUSH and is organized and managed by AARUUSH Robogyan committee.
How to join the Robotics Club?
To join the club, a formal request has to be sent to the Organizer or the Committee Heads.
Who can join the Robotics Club?
Students from any branch of SRM University, Kattankulathur campus are free to join the club if they are really passionate about robotics.
Any prerequisites?
No prerequisite except hunger to learn, sincerity to work and passion to innovate.
I want to work with the club members on a new project, how can I proceed?
Post a formal request to the Organizers heads with a small abstract which includes the details of the members who want to get involved with the project.
Best way to learn robotics?
Watch tutorials, read from the links given on the website and build things suggested by the club. Approach the members if there is any ounce of doubt still left in your mind.
How do we obtain resources for the project work?
The components can be purchased on your own or can be given by the kit partners of the Robotics Club.
What if I missed a lecture?
Refer to the tutorial to gain initial understanding of the subject and then build on it. Its important to follow up because the next lecture might build up on the previous lecture. Try not to miss many lectures.
Reasons that might add up to loss of membership?
The eviction depends on factors such as prolonged absence from meetings, classroom sessions, or other club activities without any valid reasons. Also the Organizer or the committee heads have the final say in any dispute regarding matters of eviction.
