Robotics Club

About Us


‘Imagination is the beginning of creation.’- George Bernard Shaw.
Looking back at the human strides in technology over the past few decades, one is filled with nothing short of marvel for these brilliant handiworks of man, all of which sprang as fragments of imagination of dreamers who dared to conceive the impossible.
Perhaps the most ingenious of all human creations is a Robot-a machine that can work for man, performing monotonous jobs with increased efficiency and accuracy. Robotics is one path of endless possibilities; and with man’s giant leaps bordering on lines of ‘Artificial Intelligence’, future holds a world of evolving, magnificent automation.
Driven by passion and a sheer will to concoct elements of Robotics that modernize the simple way of life, The Robotics Club at SRM University is a group of people whose belief lies to beget something beyond the extremity of chimera. It is a league that unveils to its enthusiasts, a field of study that amalgamates various tributaries of engineering.
Robotics club brings forward a large number of opportunities not only to learn and evolve but also to explore and establish. The Robotics Club has developed a catalogued system to implant interest, improve raw skills and foster it by offering lectures from experienced and sagacious personalities who possess consummate skills and have portrayed exemplary achievements in past under the province of robotics. It aims to provide monetary, informatory and infrastructural support to its revered members.
The club shall embrace anyone who wishes to annex to its missions and ambitions. For the club and all its present members the journey has just begun and the motivation lies in the several kingdoms of knowledge yet to conquer and several lands, in the field of robotics, yet to engrave a mark upon.
