ROBO BOOSTER

An endurance race filled with variety of obstacles and breath taking adventurous tasks followed by an essence of victory is all what this event is about.Participants with their bots are tested thoroughly throughout the race and based on the final results winner is taken.

ROBOBASH

Remember the race held in the annual functions in school time where we had to hold a spoon in our mouth with a lemon on top, run as fast as possible without the lemon falling. Let the kid in us come into existence once again and come be part of the lemon race but on a totally different level. There is no running, skipping or jumping here but all we need is patience, practise and better techniques.So get your batteries charged, motors aligned and be part of the next edition of the same old lemon race.

ROBODRANZER

In today’s time in the age of naval robotics when most of the researches are being carried out at water surface or beneath the water there is a great demand of water robots to perform various activities like for research purpose, ammunition, surveying etc. So, according to the need and demand we are bringing this event so as to cultivate the talent of students in the field of naval robotics. As, there is a demand of deep ocean study which cannot be performed by humans, so we can clearly understand the need of naval robotics.

ROBOFLASH

Well this is a competition comprising all terrain time lap race. Well it seems simple but don’t judge it so easily, though a single race, but its what where the toughness lies. We will not so easily let u pass by, first pass through us, then comes the actual game.

ROBOJUMP

The following is a sports event based on the game Long Jump.

In this event competitors jump as far as possible along the ground in one leap and the competitor is expected to combine speed, strength, and agility in an attempt to leap as far as possible from a take off point.

ROBO MOON BUZZER

This event is based on the game 'don't touch the line' and a round of ATV track. Wanna know more...come and enjoy the game.

ROBORUNNER

You have to design a robot either wired or wireless that can travel through all turns of the track. The robot that will complete the specified task in least time will be the winner.

ROBOSOCCER

Soccer fans taking the game to the next level with robots. A competition comprising of a player, a ball and rectangle post. The only thing replacing would be a player by a robot.

ROBOSPRINT

This is a event based on the concept of racing. Sprint is a single lapped racing event. It tests the speed management of the bot with given specifications and the control of the controller on the bot.

RoboCzar

Robozar is all about clash of Robos in two different levels. First they have to show there muscle Power and fighting skills in a street wrestling. Second round is soccer buster where robos have to show there team work and how fast are they along with their power. In short the winning bot must be strong, fast and have a skilled Operator.
