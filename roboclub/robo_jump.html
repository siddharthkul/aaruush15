Introduction

An endurance race filled with variety of obstacles and breath taking adventurous tasks followed by an essence of victory is all what this event is about.Participants with their bots are tested thoroughly throughout the race and based on the final results winner is taken.

Round description
Only 1 round

This race starts with an path filled with twists and turns followed by climbing the Everest. The race goes on by pushing the stones into the pits which is obstructing your path. We have an ramp followed by an area of sand where the bot has to travel the whole of the desert ( being the toughest endurance of the whole marathon). The race will end with bursting a ballon with the needle placed on the apex of the bot. We get our essence of the victory.
